Similar to Sudoku every digit must not appear more than once in each column and in each row.

Departing from Sudoku, neighboring white fields form streets, and must be filled with consecutive digits, in any order. Three neighboring white fields could be filled with 9-7-8 or 3-2-1. Each time a set of digits with no hole.

Note that the numbers in black fields are only hints and are not part of any streets. Streets are only the white fields.

Mouse over the board to focus a field. Type a digit while a field is focused to place it. Remove a digit with Del or Backspace.

Pass on the game by sending the URL to anyone who might enjoy it.
